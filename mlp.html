How to read the visualization

This page gives a compact explanation of the model, the PCA projections, and the latent-link views.

Model architecture

Dense + ReLU blocks followed by a two-logit softmax classifier.

Distribution gallery

The datasets are designed to stress different geometric transformations.

PCA interpretation

PCA is a viewing lens. It is not the actual classifier, but it exposes dominant directions of variation.

Suggested workflow. Start on Explore and train the model until the boundary stabilizes. Then move to Sample journey and click a difficult point near the boundary. Use Accuracy jumps to inspect which layers changed during sudden improvements. Finally, use Latent links to see how a cloud of points is transported from one representation to the next.
